Meet the characters on Among Us

crewmates

These are the players who are part of the crew aboard the spaceship. They come in various colors such as red, blue, green, etc., and wear spacesuits with a helmet.

Roles

The Scientist is an additional role added in version 2021.11.9 that was announced alongside the Sheriff at Summer Game Fest. Players with this role are given a portable vitals to monitor that can be accessed from anywhere on the map, however, the portable vitals only last for 5 seconds (adjustable in options) and to recharge, the scientist must complete a task or wait for 20 seconds, if they completed all their tasks (adjustable in options). Scientist cannot use their portable vitals during comms sabotage.

The Engineer is an additional role that was added in version 2021.11.9. Players with this role are given the ability to hide or travel through vents and cannot be killed while in the vent as a result, they can stay in the vent for 5 seconds (adjustable in options) and to gain back the ability to vent, the engineer must complete a task or wait for 20 seconds, if they completed all their tasks (adjustable in options). Engineer cannot vent during comms sabotage.

Impostors

Impostors are among the crewmates, but their goal is to sabotage the ship and eliminate other players without being caught. They also wear spacesuits, but sometimes their appearance can be slightly altered to distinguish them from crewmates.

Roles

The Shapeshifter is an additional role added in version 2021.11.9 that was announced on October 27, 2021. Players who have this role have the ability to transform into other living players for a limited time to trick crewmates with a 30 seconds cooldown (adjustable in options) lasting 10 seconds (adjustable in options). It is the only additional role that is based on the Impostor role.

Ghosts

Ghosts are role that players obtain when they are killed or ejected, their goal remain the same when they are alive as ghosts still win with their team.

Roles

The Guardian Angel is an additional role that was added in version 2021.11.9. Players with this role are given the ability to Protect living players from being killed, but only if they are crewmate, scientist or engineer, have died and Guardian Angel is enabled. Guardian Angel can use protect on any alive player with a 60 seconds cooldown (adjustable in options) for 30 seconds (adjustable in options). Guardian Angel cannot just their protect during comms sabotage.
